The view of the snowy peaks is so charming. The pristine power of the mountains is inexhaustible. Only water is able to absorb the potential accumulated by centuries and carry this energy down to the ground over the steep hills.

Eight mountain systems of Russia are eight unique places that are keeping the power of people, who lived within their ranges. Today, the centuries separate us, but we see the same snow-covered peaks reaching for the sky, feel ourselves as a part of the infinity, soak the energy of the mountains in and taste the nature of the water that carries it.

Eight Peaks water is produced in the mountains at an altitude of 998 meters above sea level, which ensures its amazing taste and pristine purity. Passing through a natural filter made of many rock formations, the water gets purified and enriched with minerals. Perfect cleanness of the water well, absence of contact with ground waters allows you to drink Eight Peaks water without additional treatment.

Source of water “Eight Peaks. Caucasus”

The fantastic beauty of a pine forest, healing air, saturated with coniferous aromas, mild climate and paragon ecology - these facts define the Arkhyz Gorge in the Greater Caucasus Range.


In this very place, at an altitude of 998 meters above sea level, lies the source of natural mineral water we called “Eight Peaks. Caucasus”.

Sikhote-Alin
Untamed wild power and
energy of the ancient civilizations
“
Abakhay the warrior was wounded and strayed for a long time, until he fell near the spring. He dipped some water from the spring and once he drank it, the strength returned to his body. Abakhay showed this healing spring to other warriors, and they rose up a temple in this place.
“
The mysterious slopes of the Sikhote-Alin mountain
range preserve the ruins of a megalithic civilization. Locals collect the roots of healing ginseng in the mountain valleys, the Amur tiger reigns in the thick woods. Swift streams run from Eastern steep cliffs into the Eastern sea, slow rivers smoothly carry their waters from the Western slopes to Amur river. Sacred lands in the Far East maintained their pristine purity, unharnessed wild power and energy of ancient civilizations.

To this day, the gigantic hexagonal columns around the Neozhidanny waterfall remind of the strength of the ancient warriors and guard the healing water.
Not far from these places, near the river Shkotovka, we make our mineral water.

Big Caucasus
Unending hospitality,
strong love, rivalry
“
After defeating the treacherous God of Lightning – Epley, the mighty Teyri has driven the stormy clouds away and ordered the God of Sun to melt the ice shell on the top of the mountain. Scorching sunrays quickly heated the ice, and clear waterfall streams rushed from the sheer walls. They sprung into the dale and quickly put the raging fire down at the bottom of the mountain...
Ever since and until our days these waterfalls are dinning over the Sofia valley,
giving birth to the river called Sofia and nurturing trees and plants at its rocky banks.
“
Unending hospitality, strong love, rivalry, tears – such fiery emotions have been running high in these places from the ancient times. The Gods themselves were feasting and battling for supremacy in the clouds of the Caucasus!

Sofia river carries its waters to the main water artery of Arkhyz – Bolshoy Zelenchuk river, the valley of which is the very source of the mineral water.

Altai
The land of the strongest
“
One of the hunters deadly wounded a maral in the mountains. Severely bleeding, the maral headed to the Arzhan-Su spring and rushed into the water. While the hunter was climbing down the mountain, the elk’s wound has skinned over. The maral jumped out of the water and ran away faster that the wind. The hunter was so stunned that he decided to throw a dried grayling into the water. To his surprise, the fish came alive and swam away. Otter’s skin was next to be thrown in the water, and the otter emerged from the waters and swam away just like the grayling did. The hunter realized that the Arzhan-Su spring bestows the power and life to everyone.
“
Altai is a land of the strong, a land of herders wrangling their flocks in the highland pastures. This is a land of hunters stalking their prey along the secret paths. Local people have been living in harmony with the nature for a long time, nurturing themselves by healing waters from the mountains.

In this very region, near Katun river, we are making the mineral water.

About the company
We strive to offer our customers the best water in Russia
At the first glance, the name of Eight Peaks is not associated with water, but it was chosen for a good reason.

Among big mountain systems, eight can be distinguished - Big Caucasus, Ural mountains, Altai, Verhoyansky range, Western and Eastern Sayans, Chersky range and Sikhote Alin. Only these places conserve the glaciers, an unfailing supply of pure water created by the Nature itself. This is where you can find the best drinking mineral water. These intact corners of the Earth are not corrupted by industrialization and even by human presence!

Our hydrogeologists carefully study the water layers of the valleys, and examine the water samples taken. A team of experts selects the best sources - water with a unique taste and balance of minerals.


However, it’s not enough to get this rare water, it's important to preserve its properties!

We bottle the water near the source, minimizing its contact with the environment.

Transparent bottle with thicker walls maintains its quality and the purity of water amazes customers from the first glance.
